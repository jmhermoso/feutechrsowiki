## General Expectations

As a member of the FIT ELITE management team, you are expected to:

* **Act Professionally:** Act professionally and ethically in all interactions.
* **Communicate Effectively:** Communicate clearly, promptly, and respectfully with players, staff, and university personnel.
* **Be a Role Model:** Serve as a positive role model for student-athletes.
* **Uphold Values:** Uphold the values and mission of FIT ELITE and Far Eastern University - Institute of Technology.
* **Prioritize Success:** Prioritize the well-being and academic success of student-athletes.
* **Be Proactive:** Be proactive in identifying and addressing team needs and potential issues.
* **Maintain Confidentiality:** Maintain confidentiality regarding sensitive player or organizational matters.
* **Fulfill Your Role:** Fulfill the specific duties associated with your appointed role.

## Specific Duties: Head Varsity Manager

The Head Varsity Manager oversees the operational aspects of the varsity teams.

### Key Responsibilities:
* **Coordination:** Coordinate activities between Varsity Team Managers and Coaches across all seasons (off-season, pre-season, in-season, post-season).
* **Issue Resolution:** Address and help settle significant problems or issues involving varsity players, both internal and external.
* **Program Flow:** Ensure the smooth operation and logistical flow for different competing teams.
* **Recruitment Support:** Work collaboratively with Team Managers and Coaches to recruit potential players.
* **Liaison:** Act as an intermediary between players and program leadership/coaches when necessary.
* **External Collaboration:** Work with the Vice President for External Affairs to explore opportunities, partnerships, and exposure for the teams.
* **Resource Management:** Ensure players are adequately equipped with necessary information and resources (e.g., registration details, player records).
* **Tournament Operations:** Spearhead the planning and execution of internal esports tournaments and events organized by FIT ELITE.

> *Reference: FIT ELITE Constitution and Bylaws - Article X, Section 1.1*

## Specific Duties: Varsity Team Manager

Varsity Team Managers handle the day-to-day operational needs of their specific assigned team(s).

### Key Responsibilities:
* **Event Logistics:** Manage and cater to the team's needs for event participation (e.g., drafting Tournament Proposals, preparing Excuse Letters, handling Team Registration).
* **Collaboration:** Work closely with the Head Varsity Manager throughout all seasons.
* **Goal Setting:** Be responsible for setting team-specific goals (in coordination with Coach/Head Manager) and ensuring their accomplishment.
* **Scrim Scheduling:** Organize and schedule scrimmages for the players.
* **Session Monitoring:** Monitor players during official training sessions.
* **Reporting:** Provide necessary reports on player/team progress or issues (e.g., monthly reports on improvements/development) to the Head Varsity Manager or Executive Committee as required.
* **Coach/Analyst Coordination:** Coordinate with the team's Game Analyst(s) and Coach(es) regarding team improvements, player feedback, and strategy implementation.
* **Player Development:** Hold overall responsibility for monitoring player training and practice effectiveness.

> *Reference: FIT ELITE Constitution and Bylaws - Article X, Section 1.2*
