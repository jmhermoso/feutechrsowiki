# Livestreaming and Technical Direction

The Technical Director (TD) manages the final mix of all video sources, audio feeds, and graphic assets into a single coherent broadcast. This section covers the TD's workflow, the network and encoding standards required for a stable stream, and the game observation protocols used across all titles the organization covers.

<Callout type="info" title="The TD is the last line of quality control">
  Every source passes through the TD before it reaches the audience. If something looks or sounds wrong on stream, the TD is responsible for catching and correcting it — not waiting to be told.
</Callout>
