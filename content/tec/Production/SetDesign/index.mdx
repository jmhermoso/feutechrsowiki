# Set Design and Fabrication

This section governs the physical environment of our productions. Our fabrication philosophy is **Student-First** and **Environmentally Conscious**, prioritizing accessible skill-building, lightweight logistics, and the creative reuse of materials.

<Callout type="info" title="No fabrication before approval">
  No materials are purchased and no building begins until the final design render and cost sheet have been signed off. This is non-negotiable.
</Callout>
