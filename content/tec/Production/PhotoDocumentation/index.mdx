# Photo Documentation

The Photo Documentation section covers all photography-related workflows within the Productions department. This includes the rapid turnaround process of live event posting and the structured, collaborative production of varsity player photoshoots.

Photo documentation officers work closely with the **Creatives department** for visual overlays and branded assets, the **Publications department** for content distribution, and **Esports Management** for coordinating varsity-related shoots.

<Callout type="info" title="Before You Begin">
  Ensure you have access to the shared drive folders for photo assets, the overlay templates from Creatives, and the approved posting schedule from Publications before starting any documentation task.
</Callout>

## Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Overlay** | Branded graphic layer provided by Creatives, applied over raw photos during post-processing |
| **Live Posting** | The practice of editing and publishing event photos in near real-time during or shortly after an event |
| **Varsity Photoshoot** | A scheduled, structured shoot to produce official player photos for publications and competitive use |
| **Varsity Manager** | Esports Management officer responsible for coordinating a specific team's players |
| **File Dump** | The process of transferring all raw and edited files to the designated shared drive folder after a shoot |
| **Phase Gate** | A checkpoint that must be completed before proceeding to the next stage of a workflow |
