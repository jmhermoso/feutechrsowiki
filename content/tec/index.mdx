## 🧭 Roadmap Overview

Use the sidebar to explore our primary documentation pillars:

### 🔰 The Essentials
* **Partners & Sponsors**: Explore our external relationships and the **Organization Primer** for a deep dive into who we are.
* **Resources**: Quick access to our **Brand Guide** and the **Constitution & Bylaws** to ensure we remain aligned with our core identity.

### 🏢 Departments
* **Esports Management**: The heart of our competitive scene—covering team management, player well-being, and university procedures.
* **Secretariat & Creatives**: Administrative standards and the visual identity that defines our public presence.
* **Production**: The technical engine behind our broadcasts, covering **Content Creation**, **Technicals**, and **Set Design**.
* **Events**: Our largest operational branch, containing:
    * **Logistics**: Gatepasses, Jersey distribution, and Room reservations.
    * **Programs**: Event proposals and program flow.
    * **League Operations**: Competitive integrity and tournament administration.

### 🌟 Talent & Leadership
* **Talents**: Our roster of hosts, casters, and ambassadors.
* **Executive Board**: Governance, high-level strategy, and organizational oversight.
