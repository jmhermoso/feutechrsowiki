## Purpose of this Handbook
This handbook serves as your practical guide for navigating the responsibilities and procedures involved in managing FIT ELITE's esports operations. Its purpose is to provide clear, actionable guidance on day-to-day tasks, expectations, university processes, and best practices specifically tailored
for our management staff.
Think of this as your operational manual. While it draws its authority directly from the official FIT ELITE Constitution and Bylaws (C&B), this handbook translates those foundational rules and structures into the "how-to" of your role. Please remember that the C&B remains the ultimate
governing document. This handbook is designed to complement, not replace it.

## Program Mission & Vision
Your work directly contributes to achieving FIT ELITE's core purpose. As outlined in our Constitution and Bylaws:
### Our Mission
FIT ELITE is dedicated to creating a harmonious ecosystem for collegiate gaming and esports. Through providing direct exposure in competitive play, content creation, and management in collegiate gaming and esports, FIT ELITE aims to produce industry-ready professionals who
combine technical expertise with a genuine passion for gaming.
### Our Vision
ELITE aspires to be an established core for the FIT student body in the industry of Esports and Gaming. We acknowledge the call to ignite the passion and the commitment of the Tamaraws to convert hobbies into possible careers and opportunities. Via exhibiting outstanding
leadership in aspects of quality of service, coordination, and entertainment, we strive to become pioneers for future gaming innovations to come. We emphasize the importance of facilitating genuine, tight-knit relationships amongst the horizon of organizations to achieve mutual goals for the
improvement of gaming and esports.

(Reference: FIT ELITE Constitution and Bylaws - Preamble, Article III)
