# Secretariat Department

**Head Director:** [Name]

The Secretariat is the backbone of the organization, ensuring all administrative tasks, documentation, and compliance requirements are met.

## Obligations
* Management of official organization records.
* Processing of event applications and university clearances.
* Minutes-taking during General Assemblies and Executive Meetings.

## How-To's

### Running Papers for Events
1. **Drafting**: Use the official university template for activity proposals.
2. **Endorsement**: Seek approval from the Department Chair or Organization Advisor.
3. **Submission**: Submit via the student affairs portal at least 15 working days before the event.

### Requesting Gatepasses
For external guests or equipment, a gatepass request must be filed 3 days in advance to the Facilities office.
