## Scope of Confidentiality
Members must treat the following as strictly confidential:
* **Internal Discussions:** All private deliberations, meeting minutes, and executive communications.
* **Player Information:** Personal details, performance evaluations, and health concerns shared in confidence.
* **Strategic Data:** Team strategies, playbooks, and non-public organizational data.

## Guidelines for Disclosure
To prevent unauthorized leaks or data breaches, adhere to these practices:
* **Need-to-Know Basis:** Only share confidential information with authorized individuals within the organization who require it to perform their duties.
* **Official Channels:** Use designated, secure communication platforms for discussing sensitive matters.
* **Prohibition on External Sharing:** Never disclose internal information to non-members or unauthorized third parties.

## Legal & Organizational Responsibility
* **Data Privacy:** All members must adhere to the principles of the **Data Privacy Act (RA 10173)** when handling any personal information.
* **Accountability:** Failure to maintain these confidentiality standards is a serious violation and may result in formal disciplinary action.
