## Core Principles

* **Respect:** Treat all members, opponents, officials, university staff, and the wider community with respect. Harassment, discrimination, bullying, or any behavior intended to cause harm is strictly prohibited.
* **Professionalism:** Maintain a professional attitude during practices, competitions, meetings, and all official communications.
* **Integrity:** Act honestly in all tasks and interactions. Academic integrity and honesty in gameplay are paramount. Avoid any form of misrepresentation or dishonesty.
* **Sportsmanship:** Compete fairly and respectfully. Toxicity, cheating, and unsportsmanlike conduct—both in-game and out—are not tolerated.
* **Compliance:** Adhere to all FIT ELITE rules, FEU Tech Student Handbook policies, league/tournament regulations, and game publisher Terms of Service.
* **Communication:** Ensure all communication is clear, respectful, and conducted through the appropriate official channels.
* **Social Media:** Represent FIT ELITE and FEU Tech positively in online spaces. Avoid posts that are obscene, defamatory, or harmful to the organization's reputation.
