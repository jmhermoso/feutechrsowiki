## Defining Cheating
Cheating is defined as any action that provides an unfair advantage. This includes, but is not limited to:
* **Software Manipulation:** Using hacks, aimbots, or third-party scripts.
* **Exploitation:** Unfairly utilizing game bugs or glitches.
* **Hardware Violations:** Using restricted peripherals or unauthorized hardware.
* **Information Leaks:** "Ghosting" or receiving unauthorized real-time information during a match.
* **Account Piloting:** Allowing another individual to play on your account or playing on another member's account.

## Consequences
The integrity of the organization is paramount. Therefore:
* **Automatic Termination:** Any player, coach, or staff member found guilty of cheating during tournaments or official events will be **immediately and automatically terminated** from the organization.

## Upholding Integrity
* **Proactive Management:** Managers are expected to actively promote a culture of fair play within their teams.
* **Mandatory Reporting:** Any suspected instances of cheating must be reported immediately to the **Head Varsity Manager** and the **Executive Committee**.
