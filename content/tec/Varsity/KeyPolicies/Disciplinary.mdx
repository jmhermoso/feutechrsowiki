## Resolution Tiers

* **Minor Issues:** These are typically handled directly by the **Manager** or **Head Varsity Manager** through verbal feedback, reminders, or informal coaching.
* **Formal Violations:** Actions such as failure to attend consecutive meetings, repeated minor conduct issues, or the dissemination of false information trigger the formal disciplinary process.

## The Warning System
For standard formal violations, the following tiered system is applied:

1.  **First Warning:** An official e-letter is sent via private message or email. This results in **Probationary Status**, typically lasting between two weeks and one month.
2.  **Second Warning:** Issued if no improvement is observed. This results in **Suspension** (ranging from one week to a full semester). During this period, the member's tasks are delegated to others.
3.  **Third Warning:** Failure to resolve the issue following a suspension may lead to final actions, including **Eviction, Demotion, or Impeachment**.

## Serious Violations & Reporting
Certain cases bypass the standard warning system to protect the integrity of the organization:

* **Immediate Termination:** Serious cases—including abuse, harassment, theft, or cheating—may lead to immediate expulsion without progressing through the warning tiers.
* **Consultation:** All penalties are decided through consultation among relevant officers to ensure an unbiased and fair outcome.
* **Whistling System:** An anonymous reporting channel is available for members to report concerns or violations safely.
