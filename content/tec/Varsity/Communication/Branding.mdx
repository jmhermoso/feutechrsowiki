## Use of Name, Logo, and Colors
Adherence to established visual guidelines ensures that our identity remains recognizable and professional:

* **Official Name & Logo:** Always use the official name—**FIT Esports League for Innovation, Technology, and Entertainment** (or **FIT ELITE**)—and the official logo. 
* **No Alterations:** The official logo must not be stretched, distorted, or tampered with. Any modifications require explicit permission from the Executive Committee.
* **Official Color Palette:** Utilize the following colors in all official materials and communications:
    * Forest Green & Lime Green
    * Deep Pink & Electric Indigo
    * Raisin Black & Ghost White
* **Mascot Representation:** When utilizing the mascot, **A.I.M.E.E.**, ensure it is represented in a manner consistent with the organization’s branding efforts.

## Merchandise
Official merchandise serves as a physical representation of our community:

* **Positive Representation:** Members should wear and use official FIT ELITE merchandise in a way that reflects positively on the organization.
* **Usage Restrictions:** Redistribution, unauthorized alteration, or copying of official merchandise through unofficial channels is strictly prohibited.

## Brand Consistency
Uniformity is key to a professional image. Ensure that the team and organization are presented consistently across all digital platforms, physical events, and written communications.
