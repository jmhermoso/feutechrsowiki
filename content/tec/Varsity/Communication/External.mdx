title: External Communication
description: As a member of FIT ELITE, your interactions with the public, other organizations, and online platforms directly reflect the values and reputation of both the organization and FEU Tech. Professionalism, accuracy, and sportsmanship are the pillars of our external relations.
---

## Communicating with External Parties
Professionalism is mandatory when interacting with the following groups:

* **Tournament Organizers:** Maintain a respectful and cooperative tone. Communication regarding registration, scheduling, rules, or disputes must be clear and concise.
* **Other Teams:** Uphold the principles of good sportsmanship. Be courteous when scheduling scrimmages or interacting during competitive events.
* **Potential Sponsors & Partners:** Formal inquiries regarding sponsorships are strictly handled by the **Vice President of External Affairs** or designated officers. If you are approached by a potential partner, politely direct the inquiry to the appropriate leadership.

## Official Representation
When acting as an ambassador for the program, keep the following standards in mind:

1.  **Professionalism:** Conduct yourself with integrity at all live events, meetings, and in digital spaces.
2.  **Accuracy:** Ensure all information shared about FIT ELITE is correct. If you are uncertain about a detail, refer the query to the appropriate officer rather than speculating.
3.  **University Representation:** Always remember that your conduct reflects on **Far Eastern University – Institute of Technology**.

## Social Media & Online Presence
All members must adhere to the following guidelines regarding their digital footprint:

* **Official Accounts:** Posting rights for official FIT ELITE channels are reserved strictly for designated individuals, such as the Social Media Manager.
* **Personal Accounts:** While your personal accounts are your own, avoid posting content that could reflect negatively on the organization, its members, or the university. This includes avoiding obscene or defamatory content.
* **Confidentiality:** Do not disclose internal team strategies, private player information, or unresolved internal conflicts to the public.
