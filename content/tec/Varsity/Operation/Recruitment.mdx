## Supporting Recruitment & Tryouts

While the overall recruitment strategy is typically led by the Head Varsity Manager, Coaches, or the Executive Committee, your role as a manager is vital in the execution phase. 

**Your involvement often includes:**
* **Logistical Support:** Assisting with the logistics of tryout sessions, including scheduling, communication, and setup.
* **Evaluation:** Observing tryouts and providing feedback to coaches and selectors based on teamwork, communication, and attitude, in addition to technical skill.
* **Candidate Management:** Communicating tryout details and processes clearly to potential recruits.

## Verifying Eligibility

No player hits the server until their eligibility is confirmed. This isn't just about rank; it’s about standing with both the university and the organization.

### University Requirements
Ensure players meet **FEU Tech** requirements, primarily:
* **Enrolment:** Must be a bona fide enrolled student of FEU Tech.
* **Academic Load:** Must meet the minimum academic unit load (Currently 12 units).
* **Disciplinary Standing:** Must have no record of major offenses in the University Discipline Unit.

### Varsity & Game-Specific Requirements
Ensure players meet any additional game-specific requirements.

> **Note:** Due to the evolving nature of various esports titles, specific requirements per game are not listed here as they are subject to change. Please coordinate with the necessary officers to create or update these requirements.

## Player Onboarding Process

Once a player is selected and eligibility is confirmed, managers guide the transition from "recruit" to "student-athlete."

**Managers assist in onboarding by:**
* **Information Gathering:** Collecting necessary player information (contact details, in-game names, etc.) for team records and communication channels.
* **Forms & Agreements:** Ensuring players receive and acknowledge key documents, including the **FIT ELITE Code of Conduct**.
* **Introductions:** Formally introducing new players to the team, coaches, and relevant platforms like the Discord server.
* **Expectations Setting:** Clearly communicating initial team rules, practice schedules, communication standards, and overall expectations.

> *Reference: FIT ELITE Constitution and Bylaws - Article X*
