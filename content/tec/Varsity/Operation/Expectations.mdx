title: Setting Expectations & Player Conduct
description: Maintaining a high standard of behavior is what separates a professional varsity program from a casual gaming group. As a manager, you are responsible for cultivating a culture of respect, discipline, and sportsmanship.
---

## Setting Expectations

Effective management starts with clarity. You are the primary voice ensuring players know what is expected of them.

* **Team Rules:** Regularly communicate and reinforce rules regarding practice attendance, punctuality, communication etiquette, and match preparation.
* **Official Policies:** Ensure players understand the official **FIT ELITE Code of Conduct** and the **FEU Tech Student Handbook**.
* **Core Values:** Explain the importance of sportsmanship and respect—both toward teammates and opponents. Toxicity, whether in-game or out-of-game, is not tolerated.

## Upholding the Code of Conduct

You are the first line of defense in maintaining a positive team environment.

* **Active Monitoring:** Observe player behavior during practices, scrims, matches, and on official platforms like Discord.
* **Direct Intervention:** Address minor issues—such as lateness or poor communication habits—promptly and directly with the player.
* **Digital Presence:** Reinforce guidelines regarding social media. Players must avoid obscene or defamatory posts that reflect poorly on the organization.
* **Brand Representation:** Ensure players follow the rules regarding the proper use of FIT ELITE merchandise and apparel.

## Escalating Conduct Issues

When issues become serious or repetitive, follow the formal escalation path:

* **Documentation:** Keep a clear log of misconduct, including dates, times, specific behaviors, and any initial actions you took.
* **Reporting:** Report significant issues (such as harassment, discrimination, or cheating) to the **Head Varsity Manager** or the **Vice President of Internal Affairs**.
* **Disciplinary Systems:** Familiarize yourself with the warning system and the **Whistling System** (for anonymous reporting).
* **Zero Tolerance:** Remember that major offenses, particularly cheating, can lead to immediate termination from the organization.
