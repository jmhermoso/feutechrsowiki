## Enabling Support Systems

Connecting players with the right resources is essential for their success as student-athletes.

* **Academic Support (Excuse Letters):** You are responsible for the end-to-end process of securing Excuse Letters for mandatory, pre-approved tournaments. This includes gathering player details, coordinating with the Secretary and Adviser, and ensuring faculty members receive the approved documentation in a timely manner.
* **Logistical Support:** By handling registrations and travel logistics, you remove the administrative burden from the players.
* **Coaching & Development:** Facilitate growth by syncing schedules with coaches and analysts. Ensure players are present for VOD reviews and strategy sessions.
* **Well-being Guidance:** Monitor your team for signs of struggle. Be ready to guide players toward university services for academic support, health, or counseling.

## Driving Recognition & Exposure

Managers ensure that hard work is recognized both inside and outside the university.

* **Official Representation:** By managing eligibility and registration, you allow players to formally represent **FEU Tech** and **FIT ELITE**.
* **Social Media Features:** Be the bridge to the Creatives and Social Media teams. Proactively share player achievements, team wins, and even personal milestones like birthdays so they can be featured on official platforms.
* **Community Building:** Foster an environment that encourages camaraderie and integrates your players into the wider esports community.

## Connecting Players with External Opportunities

While you may not secure sponsorships directly, you are the link between the players and the broader esports ecosystem.

* **Sponsorships & Partnerships:** Liaise with the **VP of External Affairs (VPE)** regarding team needs. You will often be responsible for distributing sponsored gear (peripherals, merch) to your players.
* **Exclusive Access:** Membership in FIT ELITE provides access to invite-only collegiate leagues and showcases. Ensure your team meets the participation criteria so they don't miss out on these high-visibility events.
* **Networking:** Keep players informed about industry conferences or events that could lead to future career pathways in esports.
* **Prize Winnings:** Clearly explain the prize money distribution policy. Assist the Treasurer by providing the player information needed to process payouts quickly.

## Important Guidelines for Managers

Successfully providing these benefits requires a realistic and proactive approach.

### 1. Resource Awareness
Benefits like travel or equipment depend on budgets and partnerships. Manage player expectations realistically based on what is currently confirmed and available.

### 2. Strict Process Adherence
Your ability to secure excuse letters or facility access depends entirely on following university procedures. **Proactive planning and timely submissions are non-negotiable.**

### 3. Eligibility as a Prerequisite
Remind players that benefits are a privilege tied to their standing. They must maintain their academic units, conduct standards, and participation levels to remain eligible for support.

### 4. Communication
Keep an open line with the Head Varsity Manager and Executive Committee. If you are struggling to secure a benefit for your team, speak up early.

> *Reference: FIT ELITE Constitution and Bylaws - Articles V, IX, & X*
