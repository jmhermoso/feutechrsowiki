title: Competition Management

---

## Pre-Match Preparation

Success in the server starts hours—or even days—before the first round.

* **Logistical Check:** Confirm match schedules, rulesets, and specific details with tournament organizers.
* **Eligibility & Rosters:** Double-check that all players are eligible and that roster submissions are finalized for the specific league.
* **Briefing:** Distribute opponent details, match times, and lobby links to the players and coaches. Coordinate pre-match briefings led by the Coach or Analyst.
* **Technical Readiness:** Ensure players have their accounts ready, software updated, and peripherals in working order.
* **University Paperwork:** Work with the Secretary and Adviser to submit **Tournament Proposals** and **Excuse Letters** if matches conflict with academic schedules.

## During-Match Support

During the match, you are the primary administrative anchor for the roster.

* **Tournament Liaison:** Serve as the main point of contact for tournament admins. You handle rule clarifications, scheduling shifts, or technical pauses.
* **Player Support:** Handle immediate logistical needs (water, technical troubleshooting) so players stay focused.
* **Etiquette Enforcement:** Ensure the team maintains professionalism and sportsmanship, regardless of the score.
* **Manager Boundary:** Remember: Managers typically **do not** provide in-game coaching unless specifically designated and qualified.

## Post-Match Procedures

The job isn't done when the "Victory" or "Defeat" screen appears.

* **Reporting:** Report results accurately and promptly to the Head Varsity Manager and any required tournament platforms.
* **Debrief Coordination:** Organize post-match VOD reviews or debriefings as scheduled by the coaching staff.
* **Feedback Loop:** Gather feedback from the players regarding match logistics and technical issues to improve future prep.
* **Post-Event Reports:** Assist in compiling data for reports required by SADO or the Executive Committee.
* **Prize Distribution:** Understand and communicate the process for prize money distribution to ensure transparency.
