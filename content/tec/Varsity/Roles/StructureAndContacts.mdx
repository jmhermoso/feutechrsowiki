## Program Structure Overview

FIT ELITE is a recognized student organization within **FEU Tech**, overseen by the **Student Activities and Development Office (SADO)** and guided by a **Faculty Adviser**.

* **Executive Leadership:** The program is led by an Executive Committee, which includes the President, VPs, Secretary, Treasurer, and Auditor.
* **Esports Department:** This department handles competitive team operations and includes the Head Varsity Manager and Varsity Team Managers.
* **Functional Support:** Various Directors and Committees manage specific areas like Communications and Logistics.

## Reporting Lines

To ensure clear communication, please follow these reporting channels:

* **Varsity Team Managers:** Report directly to the **Head Varsity Manager**.
* **Head Varsity Manager:** Reports to the **President** (or a designated VP) and coordinates closely with the **Organizational Adviser**.
* **Global Oversight:** All staff operate under the guidance of the Executive Committee and must adhere to all SADO and University policies.

## Key University Department Contacts

Managing a team effectively requires working with university offices.

### Student Activities and Development Office (SADO)
SADO is your primary office for:
* Organization recognition and event approvals.
* Student eligibility checks (academic standing).
* Policy guidance and general administrative support.

### Facilities Office (FO)
Reach out to the Facilities Office for:
* Booking practice venues (such as the **Game Lab**).
* Reserving event spaces on campus.
* Reporting any facility-related issues.
