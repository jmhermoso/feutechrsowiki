## Requesting Funds

Whether you need tournament registration fees or minor supplies for the team, follow the formal request path.

* **The Process:** Submit a formal request detailing the specific need and the estimated cost to the **Head Varsity Manager** or directly to the **Treasurer**.
* **Approvals:** No funds are released without the approval of the Treasurer and relevant Executive Committee members.
* **Budget Awareness:** Always stay within the team's or organization's allocated budget. If you are unsure of your remaining balance, ask the Treasurer before making plans.

## Reimbursements

We understand that sometimes you may need to cover a cost out-of-pocket. To ensure you get your money back, follow these rules:

* **Pre-Approval is Mandatory:** Never spend your own money with the expectation of reimbursement unless you have received explicit pre-approval through the fund request process first.
* **Save Your Receipts:** Keep the **original, official receipts** for every expense. Scans or photos may be required, but the physical original is often needed for university audits.
* **Prompt Submission:** Submit your receipts to the Treasurer immediately after the expense is incurred to avoid processing delays.

## Handling Team Winnings (Prize Money)

Prize money is a reward for the team’s hard work, but its distribution follows a specific legal and organizational framework.

* **The Split:** Familiarize yourself with **Article V of the Constitution and Bylaws**, which dictates the exact percentage division between players, coaches, and the organization.
* **Management:** The Treasurer and Executive Committee handle the actual receipt and payout of winnings.
* **Your Role:** Your primary responsibility is to ensure players understand the distribution policy *before* the tournament starts to manage expectations and avoid disputes.

## Financial Compliance

Strict adherence to financial guidelines is non-negotiable.

* **No Unauthorized Spending:** Do not commit organizational funds or sign contracts without proper authorization.
* **Accurate Records:** Support the Treasurer by providing clear documentation and information promptly.
* **Ask First:** If you are ever unsure about a financial procedure, consult the Treasurer or the Executive Committee before taking action.

> *Reference: FIT ELITE Constitution and Bylaws - Article V (Finance & Prize Distribution)*
