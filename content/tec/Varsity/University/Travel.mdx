## Obtaining Travel Approval

No team leaves campus without official authorization. All travel representing the organization must be approved through the proper channels.

* **Detailed Proposal:** Submit a comprehensive tournament proposal that includes the event details, purpose, a full list of attendees, dates, and estimated costs.
* **SADO Authorization:** Travel typically requires formal approval from SADO, involving specific university travel authorization forms.
* **Faculty Supervision:** For notable onsite tournaments or external events, a university Professor (usually the Adviser or a designated representative) must accompany the team.
* **Lead Time:** Start this process **weeks or even months in advance**. Significant travel requires multiple levels of university sign-off.

## Required Forms & Waivers

Paperwork is the foundation of a safe trip. Ensure every traveling member has completed the following:

* **Waivers & Release Forms:** Most off-campus activities require university-mandated liability waivers. No waiver, no travel.
* **Emergency Contact Information:** Collect and maintain an accessible list of emergency contacts for every player and staff member on the trip.

## Conduct During Travel

Whether on the commute, at the hotel, or on the tournament stage, you are an ambassador of the university.

* **Professionalism:** Maintain professional conduct at all times. The **FIT ELITE Code of Conduct** applies 24/7 during official trips.
* **Safety First:** Follow all safety guidelines provided by the university, the transport providers, and the event organizers.
* **Brand Representation:** Remember that your behavior reflects directly on **FEU Tech**. Stay disciplined, stay respectful, and stay safe.
