# Events

TEC's calendar is built around high-stakes competition and community engagement.

## General Plan of Action (GPOA) - Current Year
The focus for this year is **"Inclusive Excellence,"** expanding our reach beyond competitive players to casual gamers and digital artists.

### Major Events
* **FEU Tech Gamefest**: Our flagship annual multi-title tournament.
* **Tamaraw Cup**: A mid-year internal tournament for FEU Tech students.
* **Happy Hours**: Informal digital mixers and community game nights to boost member engagement.
