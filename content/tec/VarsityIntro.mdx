## Purpose of this Handbook
This handbook serves as your practical guide for navigating the responsibilities and procedures involved in managing FIT ELITE's esports operations.

* **Actionable Guidance:** Provides clear steps for day-to-day tasks, university processes, and best practices.
* **Operational Manual:** This translates the official *FIT ELITE Constitution and Bylaws (C&B)* into practical "how-to" steps for your role.
* **Authority:** While this guide is your go-to, the **C&B remains the ultimate governing document.** This handbook is designed to complement, not replace it.

## Program Mission & Vision
Your work directly contributes to achieving FIT ELITE's core purpose.

### Our Mission
FIT ELITE is dedicated to creating a **harmonious ecosystem** for collegiate gaming and esports. By providing direct exposure in competitive play, content creation, and management, we aim to produce **industry-ready professionals** who combine technical expertise with a genuine passion for gaming.

### Our Vision
ELITE aspires to be an established core for the FIT student body in the industry of Esports and Gaming. 
* **Ignite Passion:** We convert hobbies into viable careers and opportunities for Tamaraws.
* **Leadership:** We strive to be pioneers via outstanding quality of service, coordination, and entertainment.
* **Community:** We facilitate genuine, tight-knit relationships across organizations to improve the gaming landscape.

> *Reference: FIT ELITE Constitution and Bylaws - Preamble, Article III*
